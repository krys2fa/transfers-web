Mediation Programme

Thursdays: 20.07.2023, 27.07.2023, 03.08.2023, 17.08.2023, 24.08.2023, 31.08.2023, 07.09.2023, 14.09.2023, 21.09.2023, each starts at 6 pm.


Saturdays: 15.07.2023, 12.08.2023, 30.09.2023, each starts at 2 pm.


Meeting point: Eiscafé Dolomiti, Neumarkt 6, 49074 Osnabrück free of charge, without registration.


Guided tours of Ibrahim Mahama's installation “TRANSFER(S)” take place on Thursdays and Saturdays. Art mediators give an introduction to the artistic wrapping of the former Galeria Kaufhof building at the Neumarkt. Following a tour of the building, a discussion will take place in the ice cream café next door.

Selected guided tours are taking place with experts:


Saturday, 15.07.2023
With Bärbel Schmidt
Textile Pedagogue and Professor for textile design at the University of Osnabrück

Thursday, 31.08.2023
With Birgit Haehnel
Professor of Art and Textile Studies with a focus on transculturality at the Institute of Art / Art Education at the University of Osnabrück

Thursday, 14.09.2023
With Thorsten Heese
curator at MQ4 for urban and cultural history and initiator of the city tour “Osnabrück post- colonial”

Thursday, 21.09.2023
With Liane Schäfer
PhD student in cultural and social sciences at the University of Osnabrück with a research focus on the critique of racism, postcolonial and decolonial thinking

Saturday, 30.09.2023
With Bettina Klein
co-curator of “TRANSFER(S)”

Saturday, 09.09.2023, 15:30–17:30
