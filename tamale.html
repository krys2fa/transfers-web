TRANSFER(S): From Osnabrück to Tamale

Discursive programme at Savannah Centre for Contemporary Art (SCCA) Tamale, Ghana

Summary:

This second component of the project emphasizes the research dimension in addition to Ibrahim Mahama’s site-specific installation which will be on view in Osnabrück until 1st October, 2023. SCCA Tamale will serve as the discursive hub where the historico-political strands of the research premises are deciphered in a series of roundtables, seminars, exhibitions, performances, and presentations programmed to generate more perspectives with academics, curators, dramatists, historians, musicians, and artists.

More details about dates and participants for the public programme will be shared on this page in due time.

Local partners: SCCA Tamale, Red Clay, and Nkrumah Voli-Ni (all three institutions are founded by Ibrahim Mahama) and blaxTARLINES KUMASI

Curators: Kwasi Ohene-Ayeh and Bettina Klein

TRANSFER(S) is commissioned and organised by Kunsthalle Osnabrück/Directors: Anna Jehle and Juliane Schickedanz
